Customer Whispers

Common Questions

How far in advance should I order?

Celebration cakes require 48-72 hours. Snacks are often available for same-day pickup.

Do you support delivery beyond your immediate area?

Yes. Fransmore coordinates delivery based on order type, timing, and destination. The team will confirm the most suitable fulfillment option for your location when your order is being arranged.

Can I customize a cake to match my event theme?

Yes. You can share your colors, inspiration, wording, and preferred style, and Fransmore will guide you toward a design that suits the occasion.

Do you take same-day snack orders?

For many ready-to-eat pastries, drinks, and crunchy snacks, yes. The fastest way to confirm same-day availability is through WhatsApp.

What details should I send for a custom cake order?

Please include the occasion, date needed, number of servings, flavor choice, preferred style, colors, and any writing or decoration you want on the cake.

Do you handle party trays and bulk orders?

Yes. Fransmore handles party trays, snack jars, event pastries, and larger volume requests for celebrations, offices, and resellers.

What payment and pickup options do you offer?

Once your order is confirmed, Fransmore will guide you on payment, pickup timing, or delivery coordination depending on what you ordered and where you are located.
