Our Origin:
The Heart of the Home

Fransmore began not in a boardroom, but in a bustling home kitchen. It started with a simple, unwavering belief: that the most memorable flavors come from patience, premium ingredients, and a touch of artisan soul. What began as Oby’s Home Kitchen—a private passion for perfecting the "Crunch Factor" in traditional snacks and the airy elegance of a celebration cake—has evolved into a brand that speaks to the world.

The Artisan Philosophy

At Fransmore, we don’t just bake; we architect experiences. We understand that a Wedding Cake is more than a dessert—it is a centerpiece of a lifelong promise. We know that a jar of our Signature Chinchin isn’t just a snack—it’s a comforting companion for a busy afternoon.

Whether it’s the golden, flaky crust of our gourmet Meat Pies or the vibrant, refreshing notes of our artisan Zobo and Tigernut blends, every product is a testament to our obsession with quality. We bridge the gap between traditional heritage and modern culinary excellence.

From Local Roots
to Global Palates

While our journey is deeply rooted in the vibrant energy of Nigeria, our vision knows no borders. Fransmore is a brand built for the global citizen—the person who appreciates the fine balance of a perfectly layered Parfait and the rustic charm of hand-roasted Peanuts.

We are a growing family of creators, bakers, and flavor-makers committed to bringing a taste of artisan luxury to every table, regardless of the map. Our commitment remains the same: to ensure that every interaction with a Fransmore product feels like a personal invitation to "eat and be happy."

Our Promise

"We promise transparency in our ingredients, passion in our process, and excellence in our delivery. From our home kitchen to your celebration, we are honored to be a part of your story."
