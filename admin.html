How It Works

Publish Products With The Right Filter Logic

1. Open this admin page privately on your own device.

2. Fill in the product name, summary, image, and single-choice categories like type, collection, and order style.

3. Select the matching multi-choice filters such as occasion, preferences, volume, and bestsellers.

4. Publish it live immediately or save it as a draft by turning off the active switch. The menu page will update in realtime.
